About Us

Nova College is one of the leading institutions, offering top-notch education with modern amenities. Our goal is to nurture the leaders of tomorrow.

We offer a variety of programs, including undergraduate, postgraduate, and research opportunities. Join us to kick-start your career!

Nova International College was founded to offer practical and professional courses that enable students to navigate the globalized and open-minded economy with ease. The college aims to create a practical learning environment and develop individuals, empowering students to present themselves as professional candidates in a competitive and emerging society.

College provides an opportunity to explore subjects in depth, develop critical thinking skills, and engage in research. Independence: Students often experience a newfound sense of freedom and responsibility, learning to manage their time, finances, and personal lives.

If you have big dreams, want to enhance your career in this highly competitive world, are highly driven and want to reach beyond expectations, then Nova can help you achieve all of these. Campus Gallery

BSC.CSIT

The B.Sc. CSIT program is an undergraduate degree designed to provide students with a strong foundation in the fields of computer science and information technology. It is a comprehensive program that combines theoretical knowledge with practical skills in the rapidly evolving fields of computing and technology. This course is ideal for those who are interested in pursuing careers in software development, data science, cybersecurity, web development, IT management, and other technology-driven professions.

BHM

The Bachelor of Hotel Management (BHM) is an undergraduate program designed to prepare students for a successful career in the hospitality industry. This degree provides students with a deep understanding of the various aspects of hotel and restaurant management, as well as the skills needed to excel in a dynamic and customer-centric industry. With a combination of theoretical knowledge, practical skills, and industry exposure, BHM graduates are well-equipped to take on leadership roles in hospitality businesses, ranging from hotels and resorts to restaurants, event management firms, and tourism companies.

BBS

The Bachelor of Business Studies (BBS) is an undergraduate program designed to provide students with a strong foundation in business principles, management practices, and essential skills needed to excel in the dynamic world of business. This degree prepares students for a wide range of careers in the corporate, government, and nonprofit sectors, as well as entrepreneurship. BBS focuses on equipping students with knowledge in areas such as accounting, finance, marketing, human resource management, business law, and organizational behavior.
